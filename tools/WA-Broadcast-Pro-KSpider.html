🚀 What is WA Broadcast Pro?

WA Broadcast Pro is a powerful, 100% free WhatsApp marketing assistant by K Spider AI, developed by Gaurang Raval & Khush Raval. It allows you to manage and organize phone numbers from WhatsApp exported ZIP/TXT files, generate professional AI-powered marketing messages in 5 languages, and share them on WhatsApp, Telegram, and all major social media platforms — individually and personally.

Unlike expensive paid tools, WA Broadcast Pro works entirely in your browser — no server, no data storage, no subscription required. It is the most complete free WhatsApp outreach assistant available for Indian businesses, entrepreneurs, and marketers.

✨ Key Features of WA Broadcast Pro

📂 Smart Contact Extraction

Upload your WhatsApp exported ZIP or TXT file and instantly extract all phone numbers. Supports automatic deduplication, name detection, country code identification (+91 India, +1 USA, +44 UK, +971 UAE and 100+ more), and formatting options.

🤖 AI-Powered Message Generator

Generate professional marketing messages using 20+ styles (Viral, Breaking News, Promotional, Motivational, Storytelling, and more) in 5 languages — English, Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, and Hinglish. Powered by Claude AI with optional Anthropic API key.

📊 Smart Excel Export

Download your contacts as a Smart Excel file with 3 sheets: clickable WhatsApp/Telegram/SMS links for every contact, message + social links sheet, and a complete summary sheet. No extra software needed.

📤 Multi-Platform Sharing

Share your message to selected contacts individually via WhatsApp, Telegram, or SMS — each message opens personally, not as a mass blast. Also share on Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Reddit, TikTok, and more — all with one click.

📖 How to Use WA Broadcast Pro

Step 1: Upload & Extract

Export your WhatsApp chat, upload the ZIP or TXT file, and click "Extract Numbers". You can also paste phone numbers directly. The tool will automatically clean, deduplicate, and organize your contacts.

Step 2: Generate Message

Enter your topic, choose a message style and language, then click Generate. You can also use URL extraction mode to create messages from any YouTube video, website, or social media post.

Step 3: Share & Connect

Review your contacts and message, then click each WhatsApp button to open an individual chat window per contact. Every message is sent personally — not as a group blast. Or use the social share buttons to post on any platform.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is WA Broadcast Pro completely free? ▼
Yes, WA Broadcast Pro is 100% free forever. It is developed by K Spider AI (Gaurang Raval & Khush Raval) as a free utility tool. No subscription, no registration, and no hidden charges are required to use any feature.
Is my data safe? Does it get stored on any server? ▼
Your data is 100% safe. WA Broadcast Pro runs entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No phone numbers, messages, or personal data are ever sent to any server. All processing happens locally on your device.
What file formats are supported for contact extraction? ▼
The tool supports WhatsApp exported ZIP files, plain TXT files, CSV files, VCF (vCard) files, and LOG files. You can also paste phone numbers directly into the text area. Phone numbers in any format (with/without country code, with spaces or dashes) are automatically detected and cleaned.
How do I get an Anthropic API key for AI message generation? ▼
Visit console.anthropic.com/keys to create a free Anthropic account and generate an API key. Note that the Anthropic free tier has usage limits. The tool also has built-in offline message generation templates that work without any API key for basic use.
Can I use this tool for WhatsApp Business? ▼
Yes, WA Broadcast Pro works with both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business. The tool opens individual WhatsApp chat links for each contact — every message is sent personally, one at a time, just like manually typing it. This is not an automated bot and does not violate WhatsApp's terms of service. Always ensure your messaging content complies with WhatsApp's terms and applicable laws.
Which languages are supported for message generation? ▼
WA Broadcast Pro supports English, Hindi (Devanagari), Gujarati, Marathi, and Hinglish (Hindi-English mix). All 20+ message styles are available in all 5 languages, giving you over 100 language-style combinations.
Is there a limit on the number of contacts I can extract? ▼
There is no hard limit imposed by the tool. The display table shows up to 300 contacts at once for performance, but all extracted contacts are available for export and broadcast. The actual limit depends on your browser's memory capacity.
