❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is K Spider MultiPost really free?

Yes! 100% free forever. No subscription, no premium plan, no credit card needed.

Where are my images uploaded?

Images are uploaded to Imgur (imgur.com) — a free, public image hosting service. The URL generated is a real, permanent public link you can share anywhere. Do not upload private or sensitive images.

Can I post videos on all platforms?

Videos are not uploaded to Imgur (they are too large). Instead, your video is prepared locally and each platform tab opens — you upload the video manually on each. The caption is auto-copied.

Why does K Spider Branding appear in my captions?

K Spider Branding is a compulsory part of the tool's usage terms. It helps promote the free platform that makes this tool possible, and cannot be disabled.

Is this tool safe to use?

Yes. Only images you select are uploaded to Imgur. No data is stored on K Spider servers. Captions, hashtags and files stay in your browser only.

What is the maximum image size?

Imgur accepts images up to 20MB. Images larger than 20MB are prepared locally (same as videos). JPG, PNG, GIF, and WEBP formats are supported.
