Yes! Knowledge Spider is 100% free. It is supported by Google AdSense advertisements and affiliate links. You will never be asked to pay for basic quiz features.

English, Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Urdu, Arabic, French, German, and Spanish. More languages coming soon!

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. All your progress, scores, and registration data is stored locally on your device only. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties.
