Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 | K Spider AI

1. Information We Collect

K Spider AI collects your name, mobile number and email ID when you register to take tests. This information is used to personalize your certificates, reports and WhatsApp messages.

2. Data Storage

All student data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). We do not transmit personal data to external servers without your explicit action (e.g., WhatsApp share).

3. Google AdSense

We may display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies for personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. WhatsApp Integration

When you share via WhatsApp, the message is sent through WhatsApp's web interface. We do not store or log WhatsApp messages or phone numbers you share to.

5. Certificates

Certificates use your name as provided. They are generated locally and downloaded to your device. K Spider AI does not store certificate PDFs on servers.

6. Cookies

We use cookies for preferences (dark mode, exam settings). No third-party tracking cookies are set by K Spider AI directly.
