❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is KSpider CyberShield Pro completely free?

Yes! 100% free — no signup, no payment, no hidden charges. All 19 security modules are free forever. K Spider AI Tool is committed to making cybersecurity accessible for everyone in India.

Is my password safe when I type it here?

Absolutely. All password analysis happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your password is never sent to any server. For breach checking, we use the k-anonymity method of HaveIBeenPwned — only the first 5 characters of the SHA-1 hash are sent.

Does it work on mobile phones?

Yes! KSpider CyberShield Pro is fully responsive and works on all devices — Android, iPhone, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge browsers.

What is the AES-256 encryption module used for?

The AES-256-GCM encryption module lets you encrypt sensitive text (passwords, notes, documents) with military-grade encryption. You can safely send encrypted text to others — they need the password to decrypt it. All encryption/decryption runs in your browser.

Can students and beginners use this tool?

Definitely! CyberShield is designed to be beginner-friendly. The Security Quiz module helps you learn cybersecurity concepts, the Checklist guides you through securing your accounts step-by-step, and the Tips section provides actionable advice anyone can follow.
