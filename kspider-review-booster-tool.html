🎯 What is Review Booster Pro?

Review Booster Pro is India's most complete free tool for collecting and managing Google reviews for local businesses. It combines QR codes, NFC card programming, WhatsApp automation, multi-platform sharing, AI-powered message writing, and growth analytics — all in one 100% free, frontend-only tool hosted on K Spider AI Tools.

🚀 Quick Start Steps
1

Add Your Business

Go to Businesses tab → Enter name, phone, Google Review link → Save. All other features auto-fill from here.

2

Generate QR Code

QR & NFC tab → Select business → Generate → Download. Print on bills, standees, packaging. Customer scans = direct review page.

3

Program NFC Card

Buy NFC tags on Amazon (₹15-50 each). Install "NFC Tools" app. Write → URL → paste review link → tap card to program.

4

Send WhatsApp Requests

WhatsApp tab → Select template (Hindi/Gujarati/English) → Customize → Open in WhatsApp → Send. Best time: 1-2 hours after visit.

5

Share Everywhere

Share & AI tab → Paste review link → Share to WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Telegram, and 10+ more platforms.

6

Track & Grow

Analytics tab → Update star ratings monthly. Growth Tools → Check health score → Follow improvement tips.

💡 Pro Agency Tips
📍 Where to place QR codes for maximum scans? +
Counter table (near payment), reception desk, product packaging, bill/receipt footer, entrance door, menu cards, inside product boxes, social media posts. The receipt placement gets the highest scan rate (customer is happy after purchase).
⏰ Best time to send WhatsApp review requests? +
1-2 hours after the visit is peak time — experience is fresh. Evening 7-9 PM works well for restaurants. Morning 10-11 AM for medical/pharmacy. Avoid midnight or early morning. Response rate drops 70% if you send after 24 hours.
🌍 Which platforms matter for different businesses? +
All: Google (most important). Restaurant: Zomato + Google. Doctor/Clinic: Practo + Google. Hotel: TripAdvisor + Google. General B2B: IndiaMart + JustDial + Google. Always prioritize Google above all.
🤖 Which AI tool is best for writing review responses? +
Use the Review Response Generator in Share & AI tab for quick responses. For detailed, creative responses use ChatGPT (free tier) or Claude. For Hindi/Gujarati responses, Gemini handles Indian languages better. Always personalize before posting.
📈 How many reviews needed to rank on Google Maps? +
Tier-1 cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore): 50+ reviews, 4.3+ stars. Tier-2 cities (Surat, Ahmedabad, Jaipur): 25+ reviews, 4.0+ stars. Tier-3 towns: 10+ reviews can rank #1. Aim for 10 new reviews/month as a baseline. Recency matters — 3 new reviews this month beats 50 old ones from 2 years ago.

📡 How to Program NFC Cards

Follow these steps to program NFC cards/tags for your customers:

1

Buy NFC Tags

Search "NFC Tag Type 213" or "NFC Business Card" on Amazon/Flipkart. Cost: ₹15–50 per tag. Buy in bulk for better rates.

2

Download NFC Tools App

Install free app "NFC Tools" by wakdev (Android & iOS). Available on Google Play & App Store.

3

Write the URL

Open NFC Tools → Write → Add a Record → URL → Paste the Google Review link → Write → Tap phone on NFC card. Done!

4

Test It

Tap any NFC-enabled phone on the card — it should directly open the review page. No app required for customers!

✅ Done!
